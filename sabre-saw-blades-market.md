<p><p>The sabre saw blades market is primarily categorized into T-Shank and U-Shank types, each designed for specific applications. U-Shank blades have a traditional design, compatible with older saw models, providing versatility for general cutting tasks. Both types are essential in woodworking, metalworking, and construction, catering to the diverse needs of professionals and DIY enthusiasts.</p></p>
