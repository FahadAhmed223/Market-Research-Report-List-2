<p><p>Flocculant blocks are categorized into two main types based on their charge: positive charge and negative charge. Positive charge flocculants are typically used to neutralize negatively charged particles in wastewater treatment and mineral processing, promoting aggregation and settling. In contrast, negative charge flocculants attract positively charged particles, enhancing the removal of impurities. Each type plays a crucial role in optimizing the efficiency of separation processes, depending on the specific characteristics of the materials being treated and the desired outcomes.</p></p>
