<p><p>The truck-mounted concrete pumps market primarily includes line pumps and boom pumps, each serving distinct applications in construction. Line pumps are ideal for pumping concrete through hoses over longer distances, making them suitable for smaller projects and tight spaces. In contrast, boom pumps feature a hydraulic arm that extends, allowing for precise placement of concrete at varying heights and distances, making them essential for large-scale construction projects like high-rise buildings and infrastructure developments. Both enhance efficiency and versatility in concrete placement.</p></p>
