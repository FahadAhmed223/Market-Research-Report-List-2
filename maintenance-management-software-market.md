<p><p>Maintenance Management Software (MMS) is categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based MMS operates over the internet, allowing users to access the software from anywhere, facilitating real-time collaboration and automatic updates. This model typically involves subscription fees. On-premises MMS, on the other hand, is installed locally on a company's servers, providing greater control over data and customization, but requiring substantial upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. Each type offers distinct advantages depending on organizational needs.</p></p>
