<p><p>Magnetic stripe cards, used for transactions and access control, come in two main market types: high coercivity and low coercivity. High coercivity cards are more secure, making them suitable for applications requiring enhanced data protection, such as financial transactions and secure access. Conversely, low coercivity cards are easier to encode, making them ideal for short-term access and loyalty programs, where security is less critical. Both types serve distinct needs in various industries, balancing convenience and security.</p></p>
