<p><strong>The Security Thermal Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short-wave Length Thermal Camera</li><li>Mid-wave Length Thermal Camera</li><li>Long-wave Length Thermal Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The security thermal camera market comprises three primary types based on wavelength detection: short-wave, mid-wave, and long-wave thermal cameras. Short-wave thermal cameras utilize infrared wavelengths between 0.9 to 3 microns, offering high-resolution images but limited range in adverse weather. Mid-wave cameras, operating between 3 to 5 microns, provide a balance of sensitivity and versatility, ideal for various applications. Long-wave thermal cameras, ranging from 8 to 14 microns, excel in detecting temperature variations, making them suitable for night surveillance and long-distance monitoring.</p></p>
