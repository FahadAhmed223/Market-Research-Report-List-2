<p><p>The silicon-based masking tapes market is segmented into two primary types: single-sided and double-sided tapes. Single-sided masking tapes have adhesive on one side, making them ideal for applications where only one surface needs to be protected or masked. In contrast, double-sided tapes feature adhesive on both sides, allowing for bonding or masking between two surfaces. Both types are used in various industries, including automotive, electronics, and construction, for their durability, heat resistance, and ease of application.</p></p>
