<p><ul><li>Chilled Water Buffer Tanks (CBT)</li><li>Hot Water Buffer Tanks (HBT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The buffer tanks market includes Chilled Water Buffer Tanks (CBT) and Hot Water Buffer Tanks (HBT), serving distinct temperature control needs in HVAC systems. CBTs are designed to store chilled water, regulating flow and improving energy efficiency in cooling applications. In contrast, HBTs store heated water, aiding in thermal management for heating systems. Both types enhance system performance, reduce on/off cycling of equipment, and contribute to energy savings, making them essential components in commercial and industrial settings.</p></p>
