<p><p>Gas Chromatography Detectors are critical for analyzing chemical compositions in various applications. The market is primarily segmented into Mass Flow Dependent Detectors and Concentration Dependent Detectors. Mass Flow Dependent Detectors measure the mass of analytes as they flow through the system, ensuring accuracy regardless of the sample concentration. In contrast, Concentration Dependent Detectors respond to the concentration of substances, making them ideal for quantifying trace components in mixtures. Both types serve essential roles in fields like pharmaceuticals, environmental testing, and food safety.</p></p>
