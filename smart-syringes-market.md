<p><p>The smart syringes market comprises three primary types: </p><p>1. **Auto Disable Syringes** automatically disable after a single use to prevent reuse and reduce infection risk.</p><p>2. **Active Safety Syringes** feature mechanisms that require users to engage safety features consciously before disposing of the syringe, enhancing safety during disposal.</p><p>3. **Passive Safety Syringes** utilize built-in safety mechanisms that activate automatically after injection, minimizing the risk of needle stick injuries. Each type aims to improve safety and reduce the likelihood of infection in healthcare settings.</p></p>
