<p><ul><li>Delivery Conveyor</li><li>Injection Conveyor</li><li>Queue Conveyor</li><li>Extended Queue Conveyor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The baggage conveyor system market includes various types designed to streamline airport operations. Delivery conveyors transport luggage from screening areas to baggage reclaim zones. Injection conveyors efficiently direct bags onto the main transport line from check-in or screening points. Queue conveyors manage baggage flow during peak times, ensuring organized movement toward sorting areas. Extended queue conveyors provide additional capacity for baggage handling, accommodating larger volumes in busy airports, thereby enhancing overall efficiency in baggage management.</p></p>
