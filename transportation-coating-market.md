<p><p>The Transportation Coating Market is segmented into several types: Powder, Solvent-Based, Water-Based, and Pre-Treatment coatings. Powder coatings are applied electrostatically and cure through heat, offering durability and eco-friendliness. Solvent-Based coatings use organic solvents as carriers, providing excellent finish but with environmental concerns. Water-Based coatings utilize water as a solvent, ensuring lower emissions and easier application. Pre-Treatment coatings are applied before the main coating to enhance adhesion and corrosion resistance, ensuring longer-lasting protection for transportation assets.</p></p>
