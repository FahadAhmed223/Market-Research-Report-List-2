<p><p>The screen magnifier market comprises two distinct types: cloud-based and web-based solutions. Cloud-based screen magnifiers leverage remote servers, allowing users to access magnification features from multiple devices without local installation. They often integrate advanced functionalities, such as OCR and text-to-speech. Web-based screen magnifiers, on the other hand, operate directly through web browsers, providing magnification tools without requiring downloads. Both types enhance accessibility and usability for individuals with visual impairments by improving the clarity of on-screen content.</p></p>
