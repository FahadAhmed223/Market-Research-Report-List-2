<p><strong>The Laser Energy Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pyroelectric</li><li>Photodiode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser energy sensor market is primarily categorized into two types: pyroelectric sensors and photodiodes. Pyroelectric sensors detect changes in temperature caused by laser energy, making them effective for energy measurement in high-power applications. In contrast, photodiodes operate by converting laser light into electrical signals, offering high sensitivity and fast response times, ideal for low-power and pulsed laser applications. Both types serve critical roles in various industries, ensuring accurate laser energy measurement and optimized performance.</p></p>
