<p><p>The commercial deep fryer market is primarily divided into gas and electric types. Gas commercial fryers utilize natural gas or propane, offering faster heating and recovery times, making them ideal for high-volume kitchens. They provide precise temperature control and are often preferred for their efficiency. In contrast, electric commercial fryers are easier to install and maintain, with less reliance on ventilation. They are suited for smaller operations or establishments with limited kitchen infrastructure, offering consistent cooking performance and energy efficiency.</p></p>
