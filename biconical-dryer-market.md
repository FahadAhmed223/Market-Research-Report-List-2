<p><p>The biconical dryer market includes two main types: atmospheric dryers and vacuum dryers. Atmospheric dryers operate under normal atmospheric pressure, facilitating the drying of materials using heat and agitation, making them suitable for heat-sensitive products. In contrast, vacuum dryers function under reduced pressure, allowing for lower temperature drying, which preserves the quality of delicate materials and minimizes oxidative reactions. Both types are utilized in various industries, including pharmaceuticals, food processing, and chemicals, to enhance product quality and efficiency.</p></p>
